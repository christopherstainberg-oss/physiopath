      Everything you've entered lives only in this browser — nothing is uploaded. That protects your privacy, but it also means clearing your browsing data, or switching phone, erases your whole history. Download a backup now and again; your API key is never included.

      Ask Jeffery, Your AI Rehabilitation Specialist

      Ask about pain, swelling, ice vs heat, how hard to push, your precautions, when to see a doctor, nutrition, sleep, or motivation. Knows your conditions & program. offline

      Optionally connect your Open WebUI server for open-ended answers. Without a key, Jeffery uses a fast, private, offline knowledge base. Get an API key in Open WebUI under Settings → Account.

      Origin only (no /api). Use the host this browser can reach — localhost only works on the same machine. If PhysioPath is HTTPS, Open WebUI must be HTTPS too.

      ⚠ Key safety: your key is saved in this browser's local storage and requests go directly from your browser to your Open WebUI server (Anthropic-compatible /api/v1/messages). Anyone with access to this device could read the key. On Open WebUI, set CORS_ALLOW_ORIGIN to include this site’s origin (or *) if the browser reports a CORS block.
